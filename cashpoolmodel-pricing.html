Common Questions

Questions before you decide.

How quickly will I receive my PDF report?
Immediately — within 60 seconds of payment. The report is generated from your simulation inputs automatically and delivered to your email. No manual work involved.
Can I use the report in a board presentation?
Yes. That is exactly what it is designed for. The 12-page PDF uses a professional layout with your group's actual data, suitable for presenting to CFOs, boards, or banking partners without modification.
Who conducts the Advisory consultation?
Jakub Houser — the founder. 10+ years in treasury at ING Geneva, Citi (CEE), Accenture (Barcelona), and Erste Group. His master's thesis was specifically on cash pooling from the bank and corporate perspective.
Is this a subscription? Will I be charged again?
No. All tiers are one-time payments. Professional Report is a single purchase per report. Advisory Bundle includes one session. There are no recurring charges, no annual contracts.
Does the CEE regulatory matrix constitute legal advice?
No. The regulatory matrix provides a high-level compliance overview based on publicly available information. For binding legal guidance, you should consult local legal counsel in each jurisdiction, particularly for Poland and Hungary.
Can I run multiple scenarios and buy one report?
Yes. You can adjust the simulator inputs as many times as you want at no cost. When you are satisfied with your configuration, you purchase the report for that specific scenario. You may purchase additional reports for alternative configurations.
