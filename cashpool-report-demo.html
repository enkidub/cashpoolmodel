Implementation Roadmap & Risk Register

Implementation Roadmap
1
Bank Selection & RFP Month 1–2
Issue RFP to minimum 3 banks (recommended: KB, ČSOB, Citi or ING). Standardise pricing comparison. Evaluate pool rate, implementation timeline, and ancillary wallet.
2
Legal & Tax Framework Month 2–4
Intercompany agreements, Treasury Control Agreement (TCA), transfer pricing documentation per OECD guidelines. Local counsel review in PL and CZ.
3
Technical Implementation Month 3–6
ERP integration (SAP TRM or Kyriba), SWIFT/H2H connectivity to pool bank, user acceptance testing with treasury team.
4
Pilot & Full Rollout Month 6–8
Pilot with HoldCo Praha + Manufacturing CZ (largest positions). Parallel run 4 weeks. Full rollout to all 5 entities.
Risk Register
Transfer Pricing Compliance MEDIUM
Intercompany pool rates must be documented at arm's length. Poland and Czech Republic tax authorities have increased scrutiny since 2022. Recommend TP documentation before go-live.
Cross-border Restrictions (PL) MEDIUM
Polish thin capitalisation rules and WHT on interest may require structural adjustments. Local legal opinion required before including Logistics PL in the pool.
Counterparty Concentration LOW
Pool concentrates group liquidity with one bank. Mitigate by maintaining backup credit facility with a second bank and monitoring FDIR coverage limits.
ERP Integration Timeline OPERATIONAL
SAP TRM integration typically requires 10–14 weeks. Recommend parallel manual reconciliation in Month 1 of live operation to catch posting errors.
Final Recommendation
Proceed with zero-balancing pool implementation across all 5 entities.
Annual net benefit of €287K, payback in 10.4 months, and positive outcome across all sensitivity scenarios make this a strong business case. Priority next step: issue bank RFP within 30 days.
