Jakub Houser

LinkedIn Profile

With over a decade of experience bridging corporate treasury, banking, and consulting, Jakub founded Intercompany.io to eliminate the information asymmetry between banks and corporations. His expertise spans across optimizing liquidity for traditional global enterprises to navigating the complexities of Web3 and DeFi stablecoin treasuries.

Jakub's deep analytical approach is rooted in his academic research, having dedicated his Master's Thesis to cash pooling simulations and efficiency from both the corporate and commercial banking perspectives. He also served as a Teaching Assistant for Blockchain at the prestigious University of Nicosia, underscoring his commitment to the future of decentralized finance.
